NAVCO

The Navy Office of Community Outreach is located in the Millington, Tenn., and is a field activity of the U.S. Navy Chief of Information (CHINFO). Led by Cmdr. James Stockman, our office is made up of active duty service members and federal employees.

We connect America with their Navy.

Because the Navy is concentrated primarily on both coasts, we're challenged to communicate our mission and engage with the entire American public, away from fleet concentration areas. Our mission is to ensure the Navy's story is being told across America.

Why do outreach?

Community and public outreach is a crucial activity that reinforces trust and confidence in the Navy and its people. Outreach events and activities increase the American publics understanding of the Navy and its importance to national security and prosperity. An effective outreach program is particularly vital to the Navy due to the concentration of our bases and assets along America's coasts, and lack of exposure to the vast majority of middle America.

Get involved.

NAVCO offers an array of outreach programs and services for the Fleet to reach into markets across America that do not have a significant Navy presence. These programs provide Americans around the nation with special events and opportunities that help explain the Navy's vital importance to national security. We bring Sailors and assets to cities through Navy Weeks, hometown media, band performances, embarks to ships, provide speakers, executive engagement, Naval Aviation outreach support.
