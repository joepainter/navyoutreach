Navy Hometown News

The hometown news program highlights the accomplishments of individual Sailors as well as the worldwide activities of the Navy through local news. We provides content and interview opportunities to local media outlets throughout the nation.

Hometown media outreach exists to build the morale of our sea service personnel and helps foster public support for the people and missions of the Navy.

Although our releases emphasize the accomplishments and activities of the individual, use of the program is an exceptional way for commanders to educate the American public about a unit deployment, operation, or exercise.
