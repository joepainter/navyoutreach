NAVY SPEAKERS

Speaking opportunities

Our office can provide assistance for Navy personnel seeking a speaking opportunity. We work with a variety of organizations nationwide and can help find an event for you to speak at in the local area of your command or in cities you travel to on business or leave.

We can also assist you with your speech or presentation. Our Speakers Library contains a variety of pre-written speeches and presentations that can be tailored to your specifications.

Contact our speakers support program directory Mr. Todd Martin for additional information.

Requesting Speakers

To increase public awareness of the Navy and to improve community relations, Navy personnel are available to speak at certain public or private events. Organizations seeking a Navy representative to speak at an event need to fill out a DD 2536 request form and fax or email it to our office.

The information provided will be used to evaluate the event for appropriateness and compliance with Department of Defense policies and explicit Department of Defense policy.

Requests should be made 45 days prior to the event to allow us sufficient time to find a volunteer to fulfill the request.

Executive Engagments

We also manage the Navy's Executive Outreach Visit program and plan multi-day outreach engagements in cities for flag officers and senior executive service civilians. We can coordinate itineraries that include engagements with corporate executives, government officials, education leaders, civic groups, veterans organizations and media.

Library

Giving a speech? We maintain a library of speeches and presententions that can be tailored to suite almost any speaking event. Topics pertaining to the Navy's current high-priority issues as well as recent speeches given by senior leadership are  included.
